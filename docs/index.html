AI food photography

AI exercise videos

Short looping demo videos for every workout move, generated with Azure Sora-2 and streamed in the guided runner — with voice & a timer.

on-device

Snap to log

On-device food recognition (Google ML Kit, offline) suggests Indian dishes from a photo — you confirm and edit. Nothing leaves your phone.

🎯

Honest calorie estimates

Portion + cooking-style aware, with High/Medium/Rough confidence and a plain explanation. Approximate values show a ~.

new

Veg & non-veg, your way

Flexitarian-friendly: pick the weekdays you keep vegetarian and suggestions adapt — many of us do veg Tuesdays or Shravan.

Weight & wellness

BMI, a zoomed weight-trend line, goal-progress with “kg to go”, steps via Health Connect, and a transparent 0–100 wellness score you can see the breakdown of.
